About Me

Rick Cedar was born in Boston, Mass and grew up in a Boston suburb. The young Rick grew up an avid reader. He devoured Hardy Boys and Nancy Drew mysteries. “I am your typical die-hard Red Sox fan and still feel the 2004 thrill of my team coming back to beat the Yankees and sweep the Cardinals to win the World Series.” Rick always dreamed of being a writer. He wrote his first book at the age of nine – a story about a frog, called Frantic Frog.

First Book:

Stacked Against Us

Big-hearted, fifteen-year-old Eddie Evans, bullied and alone, meets Ilsa, the new girl in school, and together they fight to save the last movie theater in their small town.




Coming soon:

Love Conquers All

Two wounded teens. One common bond: love.

Jojo … Blonde hair. Blue eyes. Motherless. Trying to save her family’s bike shop. She has a boyfriend but can’t stop thinking about her new bike mechanic.

Damian … Brown hair. Brown eyes. Orphaned. Bike mechanic. Accused of a crime, but innocent, trying to clear his name. He’s in love but she has a boyfriend.

A story of two damaged seventeen-year-olds and how love helps them heal.
