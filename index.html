About Me

Rico Cedar was born in Boston, Mass and grew up in a Boston suburb. The young Rico grew up an avid reader. He devoured Hardy Boys and Nancy Drew mysteries. “I was your typical die-hard Red Sox fan and had the privilege of attending Game Seven of the 1967 World Series. Memories of Bob Gibson mowing down my beloved team still haunt me.” Rico always dreamed of being a writer. He wrote his first book at the age of nine – a story about a frog, called Frantic Frog.

Latest Book:

Raising Buster

Twelve-year-old BUSTER GREEN has a two-seam fastball that’s faster than any boy’s. After her mother dies, Buster is sent to live with her alcoholic, ex-major league ballplayer father who rejects the daughter he never knew he had. ​ This new town sucks. Her dad doesn’t want her. No one wants to be friends. And worst of all, the summer league commissioner won’t let girls play summer ball. But feisty Buster is hell-bent on changing all this.
